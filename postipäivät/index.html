Postipäivät

An app for displaying the next dates on which Posti (the Finnish postal service) will deliver to the chosen address.

Available on the Apple App Store.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-02-10

Postipäivät does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No data related to user identity, device details, or usage behavior is gathered.

This app does not use third-party services or tools that collect personally identifiable data. There is no data to secure, as no personal information is processed or stored.
